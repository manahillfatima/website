About the Book

Author: Elif Shafak

Genre: Historical Fiction, Romance, Sufism

Published: 2009

Synopsis: The Forty Rules of Love is a beautifully written novel that intertwines two stories. The first is about a modern-day housewife in America named Ella Rubinstein, and the second is set in the 13th century and tells the story of the famous Sufi mystic, Rumi, and his spiritual teacher, Shams of Tabriz. The novel explores themes of love, spirituality, and transformation.
